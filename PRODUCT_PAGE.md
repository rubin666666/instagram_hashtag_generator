# Product Page Copy

## Product name

TagLift AI

## One-line pitch

AI hashtag generator for Instagram that turns a simple topic into ready-to-use hashtag packs in seconds.

## Short sales copy

TagLift AI helps creators, small businesses, and personal brands generate Instagram hashtags faster. Enter a topic, choose the content format, pick a language, and get a structured hashtag pack you can copy or download right away.

## Full sales copy

Stop wasting time searching for hashtags by hand.

TagLift AI is a simple Instagram hashtag generator built for people who want faster content publishing without the usual guesswork. The app creates ready-to-use hashtag packs for posts, reels, stories, product promos, and personal brand content.

It is designed for creators, small businesses, online shops, coaches, and experts who want a practical AI tool instead of a complicated dashboard. You describe the topic, choose the content type and language, and the app generates a balanced mix of popular, medium, niche, and micro-niche hashtags.

The result is clean, easy to copy, and ready for posting. You can also export the list as a text file for later use.

- simple interface with fast output
