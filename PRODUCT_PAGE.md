# Product Page Copy

## Product name

TagLift AI

## One-line pitch

AI social media hashtag and caption generator for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Facebook, and X.

## Short sales copy

TagLift AI helps creators, businesses, and personal brands generate platform-ready captions and hashtag packs in seconds. Choose a platform, describe the content, pick the goal and language, and get a clean result you can copy or download right away.

## Full sales copy

Stop wasting time writing captions and searching for hashtags by hand.

TagLift AI is a simple multi-platform content assistant built for people who want to publish faster without guessing what tags to use. The app generates a short caption and a structured hashtag pack for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Facebook, and X.

It is designed for creators, small businesses, online shops, coaches, experts, and agencies that need quick content support without a complicated dashboard. You choose the platform, describe the topic, select the content type and goal, and the app builds a ready-to-use result matched to that channel.

The result is grouped into popular, medium, niche, and micro-niche hashtags, so it is easier to scan and use. Platform presets make the workflow faster, and recent history lets users return to past generations without rebuilding everything from scratch.

The tool is lightweight, practical, and easy to launch through Streamlit.

## Benefits

- saves time on hashtag research and caption drafting
- works across several social platforms, not just Instagram
- supports English, Ukrainian, and Russian
- includes platform presets and goal-based generation
- returns a short caption plus grouped hashtags
- simple interface with fast output
